Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026 | Effective Date: July 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Apostado Manager ("the Bot", "we", "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of the users of our bot within the Discord platform. This Privacy Policy outlines what information is collected, how it is processed, stored, and the rights you retain regarding your data. This policy is written in full compliance with Discord Developer Terms of Service and Discord Developer Policy requirements.

2. Information We Collect

To provide gaming management services, moderation utilities, and automated tournament features, the Bot processes and stores the following specific categories of Discord data:

  • Discord User Identifier (User ID): Used as a unique key to associate your gaming stats, tournament scores, blacklists, and squad rosters.
  • Discord Username and Tag: Used for display purposes within lists, leaderboards, and logs.
  • Guild Role Identifiers (Role IDs): Stored to automate role granting/revocation (e.g., Active Player roles, Blacklist roles, Staff authorization levels).
  • Guild Settings & Commands Configuration: Admin configurations for logging channels, ticket panel settings, coins rewards systems, and command authorization levels.
  • Bot Stats Data: In-game metrics including total games played, matches won/lost, coins accumulated, MVP selection counts, and blacklist history.
  • Ticket Transcripts: Temporary texts generated from closed support ticket channels containing message logs to help server owners maintain moderation records.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data processed and stored by the Bot is used exclusively to facilitate features requested by the Guild Administrators. We use your data for:

  • Creating and tracking matches, squads, and leaderboards.
  • Synchronizing user nicknames and assigning rank prefixes (e.g., RANK # | Username) as position changes.
  • Enforcing server security by tracking blacklisted users and restricting access.
  • Providing ticket channels and routing support logs to configured channels.
  • Maintaining real-time logs of point modifications and commands executed by staff.

We do NOT sell, lease, or share user data with any third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or developers.

4. Data Retention & Security

All stored variables are kept inside secure, production-grade, firewalled MongoDB database instances. We apply strict security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, manipulation, or leakage of data.

Data is stored for as long as the Bot remains inside your Discord server, or until a server administrator runs explicit data-clearing commands (e.g., resetting leaderboards). If the Bot is removed (kicked/banned) from a guild, all database records associated with that specific Guild ID are flagged and purged during our routine database maintenance cycles.

5. Data Deletion and Your Rights

Under Discord Developer guidelines and data privacy regulations, you have full control over your stored variables. You may:

  • Request to view all stored database entries connected to your user ID.
  • Request the immediate removal of all personal records (points, coin stats, active squad associations) from our databases.

To request data deletion, you can contact the server administrators of the Discord guild utilizing the Bot (who can use command lines to reset data) or open a support request in our official support server at https://discord.gg/apos. All data deletion requests are completed within 48 hours of verification.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our bot features or Discord policies. When changes are made, the revision date at the top of this page will be updated. We encourage users to check this page periodically for updates.
